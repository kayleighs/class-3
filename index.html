Student Bio


Kayleigh Starr


Kayleigh Starr was born in Nesbit, Pennsylvania. She graduated from the Univeristy of the Arts with a Bachelors degree in Fine Art. In 2014 she moved to Queens, New York. Her work explores perceptual methods of painting. She is exploring the application of coding and technology in the realm of traditional painting.
